Shipping and Returns

All prices are quoted in Australian Dollars (AUD). You can calculate costs in your country's currency at: www.xe.com Posting and shipping are done by Australia Post. You can calculate your shipping cost with our easy-to-use shipping calculator provided in the shopping cart page. We will immediately email you a confirmation of your order together with an estimated date of delivery. If you wish to arrange for Express Postage or Sea Mail, please contact us at: sales@our-emotional-health.com

Orders paid for by certified cheque or money order will be shipped once payment is received. Orders paid for by personal cheque will be shipped when cheque has been cleared. Books are guaranteed. If you receive an item that is faulty or damaged, please contact us on the day of delivery using the at: sales@our-emotional-health.com
